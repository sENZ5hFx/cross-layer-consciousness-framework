## Part I — Purpose

This document formally establishes the unified ownership and sole authorship
of all intellectual property contained in the Cross-Layer Consciousness Engine
(CLCE) repository. It is intended to:

1. Serve as a standing, version-controlled authorship declaration.
2. Provide a single reference document for IP ownership across all artefact
   types (source code, documentation, architecture, research preprints,
   patent scaffolds).
3. Formally bind all future contributors to the Developer Certificate of
   Origin (see `DCO.md`) and clarify that no contribution transfers
   ownership of the underlying system to the contributor.
4. Establish a clear public record prior to any commercial licensing,
   patent filing, or institutional partnership.
